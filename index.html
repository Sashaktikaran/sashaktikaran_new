Welcome to Sashaktikaran

Sashaktikaran Foundation is a not for profit organization based out of Jaipur.

We envision to enable children in Rajasthan with basic literacy, empower them with values and expose them to opportunities to explore themselves as well as the outside world.

We do this by creating schools into a sustainable learning system where every stakeholder- Principal, teachers, students as well as parents are collectively committed towards a collective vision and constantly learning to improve themselves for the best of children studying in school.
